FPS Bots trained with Unity's Reinforcement Learning Libraries

Developed in Unity, for COSC 698 (Applied Research in Computer Science) at Bowie State University, our team modified existing Unity ML-Agents assets to create a couple of deathmatch scenarios. This video shows one of a hundred matches on the training map to evaluate performance between varous Proximal Policy Optimization and Soft Actor-Critic Agents. All 3D models can be found at Kenney. Maze of the Minotaur

This was my Udacity Virtual Reality Developer Nanodegree Capstone Project. In it, you are a treasure hunter seeking to find 8 gems in a maze full of malicious baddies. Armed with your trusty raygun, you must find a way to exit safely with as many gems as possible. Developed for Google Cardboard on Unity, and tested on a Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge.
